-Re LED lighting system for food plant production-

 At first, a solid state lighting system  has been  developed for USA Manned Space program by a group of NASA in Houston, Texas. This lighting system is newly developed  to replace the high thermal

 profile of conventional lamp systems used by the environmental chamber industry for life science research .(http://www.quantumdev.com/LED Products/Ceres 2010.html)  Generally talking about the

 background of this project ,plants are sensitive to a similar portion of the light spectrum called Photo synthetically Active Radiation or PAR, as human eye has a spectral sensitivity with a peak around 550nm

 called C.I.E.Curve. But they are very different as shown in the attached figure which was proved with 20 crops grown in chamber by K.J.McCree,1972.  In addition to photosynthesis to creates material

 growth, several other plant actions broadly classified as photomorphogenesis are triggered by the presence or absence of light. Such plant actions are controlled by photoreceptors known as

phytochrome, crypto chrome, etc. And different plant functions are triggered in response to Blue, Red, Neared as shown in the attached table. Our Blue series(430 and 470nm), Red(660 and 666nm), and

 Neared series (730 and 735nm) has been supplied for research and development stage in food plant production business so far.  Epitex's custom-designed LEDs can cover the spectrum to be used.

-Re A Special IRLED for the Safety Light Curtain-

 The safety light curtain consists of a pair of a transmitter and a receiver. A transmitter has line of IRLEDs to transmit the light to a receiver which also contains a row ofphotodiode-based photo sensors. A

 light curtain is contracted to perform as an optical plane by aligning the transmitter and receiver. When anything crosses this plane and by aligning the transmitter and receiver. when anything crosses this

 plane and interruptthe light to one or more of the photo sensors, a detection will be made by the receiver and this message will be transmitted at once to the controller which in turn cuts power to

 themachine being regulated.  Our L870-33UP is specially designed to be intended for use in this applications of theoptical scanner such as photo electric sensor and safety light curtain.

This device features the following characteristics;

High output power(po) is typically 22μW at 20 mA.

Half viewing angle is typically ±15 degrees.

High speed response is typically 30nsec(rise) and 25nsec(fall).

Peak wavelength is typically 870nm without ambient light interference.

High performance is kept to be maintained over long term.

such a L870-33UP is an ideal product to be used for the light curtain to keep people from a hazardous and dangerous zone. And besides, when a large pulsed current is allowed to flow through itself as

 well as highspeed photodiode is adjusted to respond only to the particular pulsed light beam of this IRLED. Any change in background light can be reduced not to affect the response of this system under

 the minimized interference in the ambient light.
